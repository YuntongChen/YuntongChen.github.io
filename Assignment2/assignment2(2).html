DAUBENTON

Daubenton is a serif font designed by Olivier Dolbeau. Daubenton is a

revival of the engraved letters of the façade of the Great Evolution

Gallery of the Natural History Museum in Paris. Daubenton was the

name of the first director of the Museum.
